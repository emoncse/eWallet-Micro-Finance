F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • It is safe to save money here?

    Yes, It is safe to save money in eWallet like any other online platform. Your money is our responsibility.

  • Is it mandatory for every member to invest?

    No, that is totally their own decision. We do not force members to invest in particular loans.

  • How much time is taken to approve a loan request?

    Usually we reply within 1 day. But at most we could take 2-3 days depending on the
    loan amount and eligibility.

  • Is the interest rate actually lower than other places?

    Of course,we would never burden the loan seekers with too much interest. The interest rate will vary
    depending on the loan amount and users credit.

  • How do you decide if a person is eligible to receive loan?

    We collect all information during registration and also collect necessary documents, pictures
    etc which help will decide eligibility.

  • Does eWallet follow any guideline?

    Yes,we do. We follow the guidelines and policies set by Bangladesh Bank. All users must agree to the terms and
    conditions and we also have the authority to take legal action against any kind of deceit and wrongful activities.
